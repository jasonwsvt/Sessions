Natalie Marchant is the principal investigator and senior research fellow at University College London's Department of Mental Health of Older People within UCL's Division of Psychiatry. She obtained a PhD in psychology at Sussex University in the UK, and completed a post-doctoral fellowship in neuroimaging and cognitive neuroscience at UC Berkeley.
