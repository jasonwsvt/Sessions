Issue Resolution - Overview:

Because Reasons is a process of determination and resolution of issues using energy testing, and resolution is the use of energy testing to clear sufficiently determined reasons for an issue.

The steps to resolve an issue are as follows:

  1. Ensure the issue is sufficiently determined.
  2. The resolution command is given.
  3. Subconscious work is done.
  4. The energy tested response is provided.
  5. A yawn or deep breath may occur.
  6. A somatosensory or cognitive response may occur.
