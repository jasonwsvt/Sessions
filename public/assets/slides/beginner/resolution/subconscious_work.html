Issue Resolution - Subconscious Work:

After the resolution command is given, the subconscious then works to resolve the issue.

The way the subconscious resolves an issue is best described by elevation.

Imagine a helium balloon on a string. Resolving the issue is like taking away the ceiling. The balloon then begins to rise.

At a certain point, however, another celing may disable the balloon from going any higher.

Ideally it's a point where there is no internal conflict.

Alternatively, this point may be another issue, where a similar or entirely different internal conflict may be recognized.
