Psychological Reversals - Overview:

Psychological reversals are excuses one may have as to why one is attached or resistant to something.

Here is a list of psychological reversals and example diagnostic statements.

This list is based on one that I was given by my psychotherapist in 2009.

BeliefDeservednessDoubtPossibility
BenefitDesireIdentityPrivacy
CapabilityDeprivationMotivationSafety
ContractDeterminationPermissionShame

Each time I have added a psychological reversal, it has been when one needed to be resolved, but it was not on the list.

In those cases I was sometimes able to figure out what it was, or it was sufficient to approximate it with a combination of other psychological reversals.

Many psychological reversals often appear in groups (e.g. the identification with a behavior that's based on a belief).
