Psychological Reversals - Characteristics:

In Because Reasons, psychological reversals are not only excuses for why an issue should be kept, but also non-ideal ways one feels about the issue or the anticipated resolution, or even the issue itself.

Whenever I test for a certain reversal, I just test for the reversal name, and only determine the characteristics and associated statement if it's tested to be necessary.

Characteristics:

TargetIs the psychological reversal being felt with respect to the issue, or to some anticipated result of the resolution?
DirectionWho is feeling the psychological reversal, and to whom is it directed?
NegationIs the psychological reversal negated?
IntensityHow strong is the psychological reversal felt?
Consciousness LevelThe breadth of inclusiveness of the worldview where the psychological reversal is experienced.
