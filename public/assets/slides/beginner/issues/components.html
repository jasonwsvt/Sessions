Issues - Components

On the most basic of levels, issues are the sensations, thoughts, behaviors, or emotions that cause internal conflict.

  • A sensation is something you feel, taste, see, hear, smell, or otherwise perceive.
  • A feeling is an emotion, like happiness, sadness, anger, or despair.
  • A behavior is a manner in which someone conducts themselves.
  • A thought is a generally complicated set of feelings, ideas, memories, and images.

A good practice, especially during energy work, is to hold the sensation, feeling, behavior, or thought in mind, and try to translate it into words, being as detailed as possible.

In my experience, undesired sensations, feelings, behaviors, and thoughts, are very quick to clear with Because Reasons.

All one needs to do is sufficiently determine all the underlying reasons.

The primary purpose of these instructions is to show how energy testing can be used to determine the reasons for issues, and clear them.
