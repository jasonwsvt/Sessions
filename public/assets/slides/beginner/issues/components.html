Issues - Sensations, Thoughts, and Emotions

On the most basic of levels, issue components are sensations, thoughts, or emotions that cause internal conflict.

A sensation is something you feel, taste, see, hear, smell, or otherwise perceive.

A thought is a generally complicated set of feelings that seem to just pop into consciousness and are typically situationally repetitive.

If you hold the thought in mind you should be able to translate it into words and write them down. Be as detailed as possible, and try to recognize any associated sensations.
