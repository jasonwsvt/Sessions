Issues - Sensations, Feelings, and Thoughts

On the most basic of levels, issues are the sensations, thoughts, or emotions that cause internal conflict.

  • A sensation is something you feel, taste, see, hear, smell, or otherwise perceive.
  • A feeling is an emotion, like happiness, sadness, anger, or despair.
  • A thought is a generally complicated set of feelings, ideas, memories, and images.

A good practice, especially during energy work, is to hold the sensation, feeling, or thought in mind, and try to translate it into words, being as detailed as possible.

In my experience, undesired sensations, feelings, and thoughts, are very quick to clear with Because Reasons.

The following is an example of a verbal translation of an undesired thought, with no associated sensations, and some associated emotions.

"What is there to do? Shouldn't I be doing something? Am I missing something? What am I missing?
I'm behind, right? Behind on something? I'm going to miss something I need to do if I don't stay
alert. I'm going to screw up and everything is going to go to hell if I miss something.
I'm feeling confused and exasperated and overwhelmed."
