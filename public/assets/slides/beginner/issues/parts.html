Issues - Parts

While it might not be true that all of you feels a particular way, consider whether it's true that part of you does. It might not be something you can consciously recognize, so energy testing may be necessary to determine this.

Here's an example: I love eating the whole box of crackers.

This might energy test false, because there's a significant part of you that it really doesn't appeal to at all.

The thing about parts is that it's pretty much always unnecessary to know details about the part itself, just whether they exist circumstantially, and possibly how many.

Like this one: part of me loves eating the whole box of crackers.

This on the other hand may energy test true, and if so it'll have reasons that could be cleared.
