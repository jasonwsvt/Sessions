Energy Testing - Scientific Basis

The hard truth is that energy testing is regarded as pseudoscience by the orthodox scientific community due to it being difficult to clinically reproduce results.

That said, it has found significant inroads into psychotherapy, chiropractics, naturopathic medicine, energy psychology, and other respected fields.

So even though energy testing does not test well, it is successfully used in several professional fields to bring about healing.

It seems like the takeaway is to not expect scientifically reproducible results, or even for the results to always make sense.
