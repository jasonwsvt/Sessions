Energy Testing - The Eye Method

While paying attention to the muscles around your eyes, think or say “show me a true” and “show me a false” several times. Focus on directing the request to your subconscious. Don’t focus on what, if anything, you expect to happen.

As you continue doing this you will start noticing a specific response for each: a subtle, involuntary muscle contraction, shortly after you say the statement.

Keep practicing and the responses will become more and more evident.
