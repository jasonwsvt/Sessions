Energy Testing - Example Statements

A little more abstract than “show me a true” and “show me a false” are statements that you know are true or false.

They don’t need to be limited to facts about yourself.

Here are some statements you could try.

Be sure to also try the negation.

  1. “I’m Baymax, your personal healthcare companion.”
  2. “I live at 42 Wallaby Way, Sydney, Australia.”
  3. “I arrange, design, and sell shrubberies.”
  4. “I have a big head, and little arms.”
  5. “I'm one-sixteenth wolf with, you know, a little wolverine in there somewhere.”
