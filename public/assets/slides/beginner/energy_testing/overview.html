Energy Testing - Overview

What you say, think, or feel will cause various muscles to temporarily strengthen or weaken, depending on how harmonious or disharmonious it is to your subconscious.

Your body is always naturally responding to these stimuli in ways that can be tested, regardless of whether you're consciously testing or looking for these responses.

Energy testing is the conscious act of observing these natural responses in the body to controlled stimuli.

I consider muscle testing similar to energy testing, as they can both be used to determine whether a statement is true or false, but they're a little different in a fundamental way.

While energy testing is the observation of an involuntary muscle contraction, muscle testing is the recognition of a variance in strength of a muscle being acted upon by a force.

I use the term energy testing throughout the instructions, but muscle testing should also be able to be used in most if not all cases.

In any case, Because Reasons is a process of determination and resolution of issues using energy testing.
