Energy Testing - The Eye Method

All energy testing methods, both self- and partner-based, require testing the strength of a muscle.

Typically this involves attepmting to resist of a force, and observing the difference in strength in the shoulder muscle, or in muscles in the hands.

I use a self testing method that my psychotherapist taught me in 2009 in which I observe responses in muscles around my eyes.

This method is different from typical ones in that it requires observing a subtle sensation caused by an involuntary contraction of muscles around the eyes, and involves no resistance of any force.

Benefits of this method include the following:

  • Hands are free for other uses.
  • After a short period of conditioning, muscle fatigue is not an issue.
  • More details exist in responses than just true and false.
