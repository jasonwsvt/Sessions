Energy Testing - The Eye Method

I use a self testing method that my psychotherapist taught me in 2009 in which I observe responses in muscles around my eyes. Most energy testing methods, both self- and partner-based, require testing the strength of a muscle. The eye method is a little different in that it requires observing an involuntary contraction of muscles around the eyes. Benefits of this method include the following:

  • Hands are free for other uses.
  • After a short period of conditioning, muscle fatigue is not an issue.
  • More details exist in responses than just true and false.
