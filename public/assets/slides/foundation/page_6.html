Issue specification is the process of starting with a statement that’s representative of an issue, and adjusting the words contained in the statement until it energy tests sufficiently true. Perhaps you’re starting out with “the world is a scary place” but end up with “the world is a really scary place.” You might even end up with a second issue - “the world is an extremely scary place.” Each adjustment can be quickly determined from a list I’ve painstakingly developed through trial and error since 2009.
