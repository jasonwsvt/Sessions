Energy Testing - Avoiding Influence

Any desires or beliefs you have regarding the truth or falsity of a statement can and do influence the energy tested response. So it’s really important to not influence the results with your desires and beliefs. I call this getting out of your own way. You might need to begin testing with something like "In the name of the highest good," or “Source knows that every part of me feels, believes, knows, and practices the following:” in order to increase the accuracy of an answer.
