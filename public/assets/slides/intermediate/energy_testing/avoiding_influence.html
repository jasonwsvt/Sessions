Energy Testing - Avoiding Influence

Any desires or beliefs you have regarding the truth or falsity of a statement can and do influence the energy tested response. So it’s really important to not influence the results with your desires and beliefs. I call this getting out of your own way. To increase the accuracy of an answer, one might need to begin testing with something like "In the name of the highest good," or “Every part of me feels / believes / knows / practices ...”
