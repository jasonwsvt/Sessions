Energy Testing - Statement Semantics

Consider the following statement:

“I never said they stole my money.”

The meaning of this statement changes depending on which words have emphasis.

Consider the implication of saying it in one of the following ways:

StatementPossible implication
“I never said they stole my money.”Perhaps it happened but this person kept quiet?
“I never said they stole my money.”Perhaps someone else stole it?
“I never said they stole my money.”Perhaps this person gave it to them?
“I never said they stole my money.”Perhaps they stole something else?

Similar consideration must be taken when putting together statements to energy test.
