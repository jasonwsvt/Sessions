Issue Determination - Stacks:

In my energy work I've seen an interesting aspect of attachment that I've termed stacking.

I'm unsure whether it occurs with any other psychological reversal, or any other aspect of energy work.

A two attachment stack, for instance, is an attachment to an attachment.

Attachment stacks can be small, or exceptionally high in number.

Often an amount in the pentillion-illions tests true.

But in my experience, it's usually sufficiently true to determine number of attachment stacks along with the number of attachments in each stack.

Once these have been determined, the speed at which an issue clears has been quite remarkable.
