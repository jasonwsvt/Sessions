Numbers - Between One and a Million or so:

When I need to find a number and the range of that number is unknown, here's the general process I use:

  1. Determine the precision necessary for sufficient determination.
  2. Determine the range maximum by finding the lowest number in following list that's above they one you're searching for:
    ten, one hundred, one thousand, ten thousand, one hundred thousand, one million, ten million, one hundred million, etc.
  3. Determine the maximum number for the range by doing the following:
    1. Find the entry that follows the minimum number in the range.
    4. is just less than the following entry.
    For example, if the precision was 2 and the the minimum was 1 (really 1.0), the maximum would be 9.9.
  4. Use the binary search method to find the number.
