Numbers - Between One and a Million or so:

When I need to find a number and the range of that number is unknown, here's the general process I use:

  1. Determine the precision necessary for sufficient determination. The precision is always between 0 and 12.
  2. The range minimum is 0. Determine the range maximum by energy testing the lowest number in following list that's above the one you're searching for:
    OneThousandMillion
    1,0001,000,000
    1010,00010,000,000
    100100,000100,000,000
  3. If the precision isn't 0, use the binary search method to find the number.
