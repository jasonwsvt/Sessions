Issues - Parts:

We covered parts in the beginner section, but there are some more advanced statements that could be used when figuring out a statement to use as an issue.

These statements are more general and will pick up more reasons if there are any.

  • "A number of parts of me are driven in some way to do / think / feel this thing."
  • "Parts of me are driven in any way to eat more than a minimally necessary amount."
