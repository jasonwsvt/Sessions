Issues - Example

For an example I'm going to find an affirmation that really resonates with me.

From the Positive Affirmations site I referenced previously, I chose "My mind and heart are full of joy."

I chose it not only because I feel my mind and heart could be more full of joy, but also because of a big tail-ender I felt when I was considering it. Of course it's just an instantaneous thought that popped into consciousness, but putting it into words I get "what will people think of me when they find out I'm not full of joy already?"

So first I'm going to clear the tail-ender, because that's really important - it'll stop an issue from clearing if it's strong enough.

And then afterward I'll clear the resistance to the affirmation.
