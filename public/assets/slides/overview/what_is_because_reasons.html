What is Because Reasons?

Because Reasons is a process of determination and resolution of issues using energy testing.

This series of slides is organized into levels, concepts, and sections. There are four levels - Overview, Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced. Each level further explains the concepts of issues, energy testing, determination, and resolution, as they apply to the process of using the energy psychology method, Because Reasons. The remaining slides in this level will cover the very basics of each of these concepts.
