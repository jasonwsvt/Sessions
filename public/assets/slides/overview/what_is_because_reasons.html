What is Because Reasons?

Because Reasons is an energy psychology method that uses energy testing to determine and resolve issues.

To best explain the concepts of issues, energy testing, determination, and resolution, I've divided them up into four different levels:

  1. overview - a one-slide synopsis of each concept,
  2. beginner - tangible things that everone is familiar with,
  3. intermediate - well-understood concepts in the field of subtle energy, and
  4. advanced - things I've discerned that that aren't very well understood but still contribute to healing.

Any reader should be able to stop after any level and be fairly confident in their ability to practice Because Reasons at that level.

The remaining slides in this level will cover the very basics of each of these concepts.


Its namesake is a slang term that one may say to avoid specifying actual reasons, implicitly because they're embarrassing, complicated, too revealing, offensive, uncertain, or unknown.

It's meant as somewhat of a quadruple entendre:

  1. Someone may not want to tell someone else about an issue they're experiencing.
    Person 1: Why are you so afraid of clowns? Person 2: Ugh... Because reasons.
  2. As I will explain later, issues exist as a consequence of their underlying reasons.
    Person 1: Why are you so afraid of clowns? Person 2: Because reasons.
  3. As I will explain later, this method helps someone discover all the unknown or unclear reasons for why they experience an issue.
    Person 1: Why are you so afraid of clowns? Person 2: Because reasons!
  4. As I will explain later, this method helps someone clear all the reasons for issues they experience.
    Person 1: Hey, you aren't afraid of clowns anymore! Why's that? Person 2: Because Reasons!
