Issue Determination - A Definition:

As you may have already recognized, there seems to be an implicit connection between consciousness level and emotion. But generally speaking, every thought, action, and thing has a consciousness level. So this includes people, places, organizations, parts of the body, behavioral patterns, and psychological reversals. Just about the only thing that doesn't have a consciousness level are senses. Consciousness levels are especially convenient when determining emotions as we will see. Otherwise, they aren’t required for sufficient determination all that often.

Hawkins uses the term “level of consciousness” to describe a certain quality of a person, people, or their beliefs, actions, or creations, but as far as I can tell, there is not an explicit definition for it. I would like to provide the following one:

Consciousness level:

“The breadth of inclusiveness of the worldview from which consciousness is expressed.”

By this I mean that the consciousness level of someone or something is directly correlated with their expressed or represented acceptance of, and identity with, other.
