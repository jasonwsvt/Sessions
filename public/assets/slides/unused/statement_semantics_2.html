Energy Testing - Statement Semantics

Consider the following statement:

“My subconscious is very literal.”

Testing this statement may result in a false response because your subconscious may consider itself 98% literal, which corresponds to extremely, or very literal (note the italics). Keep in mind here the importance of understanding how minute changes in emphasis and word usage will change the meaning perceived by one's subconscious, perhaps quite significantly. The percentages shown here may not be exactly the same for others. If adjectives aren’t sufficient enough, consider using percentages.

Without emphasis Percentage With Emphasis Percentage
Extremely 98-100% Extremely 99.7-100%
Very 69-78% Very 89%-98%
Somewhat 47-62% Somewhat 39-65%
Not really 16-31% Not really 14-28%
Not at all 0-3% Not at all 0-0.2%
