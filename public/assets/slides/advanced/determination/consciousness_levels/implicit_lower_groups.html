Issue Determination - Implicit Lower Groups:

Note how every group transcends all those before it. As the distance between groups increases, fewer and fewer of the aspects are maintained. For example, there are more common aspects of anger in pride, than of fear. There is reason in love, though it may not be able to be understood.
