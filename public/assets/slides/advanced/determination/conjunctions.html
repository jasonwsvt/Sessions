I have been able to glean a little regarding how some of these conjunctions work:

  • When conjunctions connect components it is like the components are either broken or enmeshed.
  • Energy healing for conjunct terms heals them so that they are no longer broken, or at least less so.
  • While the term "and" groups two components, "&" is more of an inclusive range between the two components.
  • "Bang" (!) works with all emotions from the emotion or consciousness level specified down to 0.
  • "At" works with one consciousness level.
  • "above" works with the range of consciousness levels just above the given one to infinity.
  • "below" works with the range of consciousness levels just below given one down to zero.
  • "between" works with the range of consciousness levels between but not including the given two.
  • "at/above" works with the range of consciousness levels from the given one to infinity.
  • "at/below" works with the range of consciousness levels from the given one down to zero.
  • "at/between" works with the range of consciousness levels between and including the given two.
