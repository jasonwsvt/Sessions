Numbers - Very Small and Large Numbers:

Previously we learned how to determine a number between one a million or so.

What about an especially large number, or a particularly small number between 0 and 1?

For these I use scientific notation, where the range is always 0 < X < 10.

Here's the general process I use:

  1. Determine the power, then the precision.
  2. Use the binary search method to determine the number.
