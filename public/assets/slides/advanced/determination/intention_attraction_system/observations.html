Issue Determination - Intention / Attraction System

Some observations I've made about this system are shown below.

It might be beneficial to move between this slide and the next while reading the following points.

  • The system is an eight tier by fourteen level grid to which an individual's chakras connect.
  • Each tier has a minimum consciousness level that is acceptable for intention.
    As the tier increases, the minimally acceptable consciousness level decreases.
  • Each level is associated with one chakra, for which an individual has fourteen.
  • Levels 1 - 7 are associated with an individual's self, and correlate to the seven chakras with which we are familiar.
    As an individual controls their intention, they control the placement of their lower seven chakras.
  • levels 8 - 14 and their associated chakras are associated with an individual's experience.
    The individual's subconscious controls their placement, and my understanding is that it's based upon desired learning.
  • An individual's experiences are shaped by each chakra connection.
  • My understanding as to why tiers 4 and 5 have the same minimally accepted intention consciousness level is that tier 4 pertains to thoughts, and tier 5 pertains to actions.
  • Chakras can have connections within one tier, or two adjacent tiers.
  • Adjacent chakras must have at least one connected tier in common.
  • A new attraction can be strong enough to break previously set connections, and will do so if necessary to maintain the previous two rules.
