Issue Determination - Intention / Attraction System

My understanding of this system is not complete, but it has grown as I have continued to clear issues with portions of it as components.

Every individual has a subtle connection to everything with which they identify, to which they are attached, and with which they interact.

Each of these subtle connections has an intrinsic consciousness level.

The consciousness level of the connection is the consciousness level of the intention of the identification, attachment, or interaction.

An intention is the desired result an individual has for the connection.

The Intention / Attraction System connects individuals to a source of further attraction to experiences similar to the consciousness level of the intention.

The system causes experiences in that consciousness level range to be attracted to the individual until the connection is cleared.

Strong connections will prevail over weak ones, until the individual has the conscious intention to release them.

When a connection is cleared, it doesn't mean the repercussions of the initial event will stop manifesting.
