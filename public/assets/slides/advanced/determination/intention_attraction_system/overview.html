Issue Determination - Intention / Attraction System

My understanding of this system is not complete, but it has grown as I have continued to clear issues with portions of it as components.

Every individual has a subtle connection to everything with which they identify, to which they are attached, and with which they interact.

The consciousness level with which an individual connects to something has to do with their intention: the desired result an individual has for the identification, attachment, or interaction.

Once the individual is connected, the Intention / Attraction System then provides individuals with experiences of similar consciousness level to that of the intention.

Experiences in that consciousness level range are attracted to the individual until the connection is broken or released.

Strong connections will prevail over weak ones, but no connection is stronger than the conscious intention to release it.

As with all issues, sufficient determination of the characteristics of the connection is required for its release.

When a connection is established, it sets in motion various consequences to the action.

While the consequences of a connection continue to occur after the connection's release, the attraction to new experiences of similar consciousness level does not.
