Numbers - Very Small and Large Numbers:

Here's an example: 5,865,459,347,082 (5.865459347082 x 1012), sufficient precision: 3.

  1. Determine each digit of the power (usually -1000 - 1000).
    Negative: False; 0-500: True; 0-250: True; 0-125: True; 0-65: True; 0-33: True; 0-17: True; 0-9: False; 10-13: True; 10-11: False; 12: True.
  2. Determine the precision (usually 0 - 12).
    0-6: True; 0-3: True; 0-1: False; 2: False; 3: True.
  3. Determine each digit of the mantissa, to the required precision.
    1: 0-5: True; 0-2: False; 3: False; 4: False; 5: True.
    2: 0-5: False; 6-7: False; 8: True.
    3: 0-5: False; 6-8: True; 6: False; 7: True.

Determined number: 5.87 x 1012
