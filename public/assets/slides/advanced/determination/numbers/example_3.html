Numbers - Very Small and Large Numbers:

And here's yet another example: .0000000000000999999999 (9.99999999 * 10^-14), sufficient precision: 5.

  1. Determine each digit of the power (usually -1000 - 1000).
    Negative: True; 0-500: True; 0-250: True; 0-125: True; 0-65: True; 0-33: True; 0-17: True; 1-9: False; 10-13: False; 14-15: True; -14: True.
  2. Determine the precision (usually 0 - 12).
    0-6: True; 0-3: False; 4: False; 5: True.
  3. Determine each digit of the mantissa, to the required precision.
    1: 0-5: False; 6-7: False; 8: False; 9: True.
    2: 0-5: False; 6-7: False; 8: False; 9: True.
    3: 0-5: False; 6-7: False; 8: False; 9: True.
    4: 0-5: False; 6-7: False; 8: False; 9: True.
    5: 0-5: False; 6-7: False; 8: False; 9: True.

Determined number: 9.9999 x 10-14
