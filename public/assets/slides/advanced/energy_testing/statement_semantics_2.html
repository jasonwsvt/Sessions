Energy Testing - Statement Semantics

Consider the following statement:

“My subconscious is very literal.”

Testing this statement may result in a false response because your subconscious may consider itself extremely, or very literal (note the italics).

If adjectives aren’t sufficient enough, consider using percentages.

The following is a list of words that represent magnitudes, and the corresponding percentage range that energy tests true as representative of that word.

On the right I show how saying or thinking the same word with emphasis changes the representative range.
