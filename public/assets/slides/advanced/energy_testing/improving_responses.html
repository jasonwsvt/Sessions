Energy Testing - Improving Responses

If a response is not a solid true or false, it means that what you are testing needs to be adjusted in some way. Declarative statements are the best things to energy test, along with thoughts that can be easily translated to declarative statements. If the energy tested response to a declarative statement isn’t a solid true or false, it’s likely that the statement needs to be adjusted in some way. Typically for me, an adjective that describes a magnitude needs to be changed. Regardless, however, you can use energy testing to determine how the statement needs to be adjusted.
