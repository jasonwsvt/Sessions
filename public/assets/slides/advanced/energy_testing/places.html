Numbers - Determining the number of places in a larger number:

Previously we learned how to determine a number under a million or so. What about an even larger number? The range in this case would be X*10^N - (X+1)*10^N.

Here's the general process I use:

1) Determine the values for N, then X.

2) Determine the value for X.

3) Determine the required precision.

4) User the binary search method to determine the number.
